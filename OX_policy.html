Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information directly from users.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information to provide better services and advertisements.

Third-Party Services

XO Arena uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and use certain information, including but not limited to:

This data is used to show personalized or non-personalized ads and to improve ad performance.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Ads

This app contains advertisements. Ads are provided by Google AdMob.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by enabling the appropriate settings on their device:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads

In case of an error in the app, we may collect log data through third-party services. This log data may include device information, operating system version, app configuration, and usage statistics.

Cookies

XO Arena does not use cookies directly. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies.

Children’s Information

XO Arena is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
